Welcome to Pie Hole Pizzeria

More Than Just Pizza

Tucked away on 15th Street in Tulsa, Pie Hole Pizzeria has been serving up handcrafted pies, fresh salads, and hearty calzones that keep our neighbors coming back. From classic pepperoni to adventurous gourmet creations, every pie is made with love — and a whole lot of cheese.
